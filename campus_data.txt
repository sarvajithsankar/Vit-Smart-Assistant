VIT VELLORE CAMPUS GUIDE - 2025

1. WIFI ACCESS (VIT-Connect):
   - Network Name: "VIT 2.4G" or "VIT 5G"
   - Login: Use your Registration Number (e.g., 23BCE...) and WiFi Password.
   - Shutdown Time: WiFi turns OFF automatically from 12:30 AM to 4:30 AM every night.
   - Monthly Limit: 100 GB per student (resets on the 1st of the month).

2. PERIYAR LIBRARY (The Central Library):
   - Location: Opposite the Main Building (MB).
   - Weekday Hours: 7:30 AM - 12:00 Midnight (Mon-Fri).
   - Weekend Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Sat-Sun).
   - During Exams: Open until 1:00 AM.
   - Rules: "Bag Check" is mandatory at the entrance.

3. FOOD & CANTEENS:
   - TT Canteen (Technology Tower): Best for Fried Rice and Fresh Juice.
   - SJT Canteen (Silver Jubilee Tower): Famous for Sandwiches and Coffee.
   - GDN Canteen: Located near the G.D. Naidu Block.
   - Domino's Pizza: Located inside the campus near the gate.
   - "Paid Mess": Available for students who want a change from regular mess food.
