### VIT VELLORE CAMPUS GUIDE - 2026

1. WIFI ACCESS (VIT-Connect):
    - Network Name: "VIT 2.4G" or "VIT 5G"
    - Login: Use your Registration Number and WiFi Password.
    - Shutdown Time: WiFi turns OFF automatically from 12:30 AM to 4:30 AM.
    - Monthly Limit: 100 GB per student (resets on the 1st of the month).
    - Policy: Managed via the student portal; ensure device registration is updated.

2. PERIYAR LIBRARY (The Central Library):
    - Location: Opposite the Main Building (MB).
    - Weekday Hours: 7:30 AM - 12:00 Midnight (Mon-Fri).
    - Weekend Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Sat-Sun).
    - During Exams: Open until 1:00 AM.
    - Rules: "Bag Check" is mandatory; ID card required for entry.

3. FOOD & CANTEENS:
    - TT Canteen: Best for Fried Rice and Fresh Juice.
    - SJT Canteen: Famous for Sandwiches and Coffee.
    - GDN Canteen: Located near the G.D. Naidu Block.
    - Food Courts: Various outlets available across campus blocks.
    - Domino's Pizza: Located inside the campus near the gate.
    - Paid Mess: Available for students opting for varied meal plans.

4. EMERGENCY CONTACTS:
    - Campus Security (Main Gate): 0416-220-2527
    - Health Centre (Ambulance): 0416-220-2217 (AL Mudaliar Block).
    - Men's Hostel Office: 0416-220-2520
